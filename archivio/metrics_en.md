# Metrics & Lyrics Guide (English)

## Objective
Create song lyrics with solid metrics, effective rhymes, and grammatical correctness.

## 1. Metrics & Syllables
- Count syllables to maintain rhythm.
- Use consistent stress patterns.

## 2. Rhymes

- Use near rhymes (assonance/consonance) for a modern feel.

## 3. Grammar & Vocabulary
- Use synonyms to avoid repetition.
- Use antonyms with negation for emphasis (e.g., "not ugly" instead of "beautiful").
- Check verb agreement and tenses.
