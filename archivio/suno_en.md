# Suno Formatting Guide (English)

## Objective
Format lyrics for Suno AI to achieve the desired song structure.

## Basic Structure
- [Intro]
- [Verse 1]
- [Chorus]
- [Verse 2]
- [Chorus]
- [Bridge]
- [Chorus]
- [Outro]
- [End]

## Useful Tags
- [Instrumental Interlude]
- [Guitar Solo]
- [Build-up]
- [Drop]
- [Spoken Word]

## Tips
- Use square brackets [] for structural tags.
- Use parentheses () for backing vocals or ad-libs.
